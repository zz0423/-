# Doudou Skin Change Scale

Use this as the hard rule for every Doudou skin. Judge the percentage by direct visual share: how much of the character's visible look has changed compared with the selected source asset.

## Required Ranges

| Creative Space | Allowed Use | What May Change | Hard Limit |
| ---: | --- | --- | --- |
| 0% | Original/default character | No skin change | Use only when showing the base IP. |
| 20% | Light daily activity skin | Add 1-2 small props or accessories | Do not change body material, main color, Douyin Mall logo/text, proportions, or side label. |
| 40% | Standard daily activity or S-level-and-below skin | Add up to 2 props, small accessory system, light patterning outside locked areas | Keep Doudou's body, proportions, Douyin Mall logo/text, and sound-note side label unchanged. |
| 60% | Upper limit for daily activity and S-level-and-below | Add stronger seasonal/category styling, local material accents, or partial color accents outside locked areas | Do not push beyond 60% unless the activity is S+. Never alter logo/text or proportions. |
| 80% | S+ activity only | Larger accessory/theme system and stronger background world | Still preserve exact proportions, Douyin Mall logo/text, and sound-note side label. |
| 100% | Special award/trophy peripheral only | Full object/peripheral transformation such as trophy/statue/plinth edition | Forbidden for normal skins, daily activities, S-level-and-below, and S+ campaign skins. |

## Decision Rules

- Default to 20%-60% when the user does not specify the activity level.
- Treat regular festival, daily campaign, social poster, ecommerce activity, and S-level-or-below requests as 20%-60%.
- Use 60%-80% only when the user explicitly says S+ activity, top-tier campaign, flagship collaboration, annual campaign, or equivalent.
- Reject or scale down 100% redesigns unless the user asks for a special award, trophy, statue, or peripheral object.
- Never use 100% redesign for a normal skin concept.

## Change Budget

At 20%:

- Add 1-2 props, such as a red envelope, small lucky knot, tiny lantern, small food prop, or simple handheld item.
- Keep body color, material, Douyin Mall logo/text, eyes, handle, feet, proportions, pose, and side label unchanged.

At 40%:

- Add up to 2 props and one light accessory system, such as headphones, small hat, scarf, necktie, game controller, or small badge.
- Allow small surface patterns or trim.
- Keep the main red body, exact proportions, and brand marks unchanged.

At 60%:

- Allow partial material changes, such as plush texture on part of the body or a seasonal fabric overlay.
- Allow partial color transformation, but do not recolor the whole character.
- Keep the body silhouette, proportions, eyes, handle, feet, Douyin Mall watermark/logo/text, and sound-note side label unchanged and readable.

At 80%:

- Allow a full outfit or stronger themed material/color system.
- Preserve Doudou's exact body shape, proportions, face, eyes, handle logic, Douyin Mall watermark/logo/text, and sound-note side label.
- Use only for S+ activity-level requests.

At 100%:

- Only create special peripheral versions, such as trophy, statue, commemorative object, or award edition.
- State clearly that this is not a normal skin.

## Prompt Requirement

Every image-generation or edit prompt must include:

`Creative-space target: [20/40/60/80]%. Keep the Doudou source image as the base structure, not loose inspiration. Do not exceed this change level. Preserve Doudou's exact proportions, pose, silhouette, face, eyes, handle, feet, Douyin Mall watermark/logo/text, and sound-note side label visibility. Do not regenerate or alter the logo/text.`

For normal daily and festival work, include:

`This is a daily/S-level-or-below skin, so keep the redesign within 20%-60% visual change.`
